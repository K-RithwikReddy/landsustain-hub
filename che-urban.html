Types of Urbanization and Infrastructure Development

Residential Urbanization

Residential urbanization involves the expansion of housing and living spaces within urban areas to accommodate population growth and migration.

Construction of housing complexes, apartments, and residential communities.

Transportation Infrastructure Development

Development of transportation networks to facilitate the movement of people and goods within urban areas.

Building roads, highways, bridges, railways, public transit systems (subways, buses, trams), and airports

Utilities and Services Infrastructure

Establishing essential utilities and services within urban areas, such as water supply, sewage systems, waste management, electricity, and telecommunications.

Installation of water supply systems, sewage systems, waste management facilities, electricity grids, and telecommunications networks.
