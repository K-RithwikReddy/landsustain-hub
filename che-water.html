Causes of Water Scarcity

Climate change

Climate change is altering weather patterns around the world, leading to more extreme weather events such as droughts and floods.

Droughts can reduce water availability in surface and groundwater sources, while floods can damage water infrastructure
and contaminate water supplies.

Overuse and wastage of water

Overuse and wastage of water is a major problem in many parts of the world. This can be due to inefficient irrigation practices,
leaky pipes, and consumer behavior such as long showers and running the tap while brushing teeth.

Population growth

Population growth is putting increasing pressure on water resources. As more people need water, there is less to go around.

This is especially true in developing countries, where populations are growing rapidly and water infrastructure is often inadequate.
